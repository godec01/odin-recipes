Cokolino

Cokolino has been in the Balkans a favorite food for the whole family for generations. Excellent cereal porridge with a chocolate flavor, in combination with 2 dl of milk, instantly turns into a nutritious and tasty meal.

    Ingredients you need:

  • You only need cokolino. It is the esscence of life

      • How to become worthy of eating cokolino?

  • Put cokolino in a bowl
  • Pour milk on top of it

    • Note: Pouring milk before cereal disrupts the natural order of breakfast preparation. It prevents proper cereal-to-milk ratios, compromises texture, and creates unnecessary splashing. Such behavior should be discouraged, if not outright condemned, to preserve the integrity of this cherished morning ritual.
